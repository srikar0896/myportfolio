A platform that leverages digital twin technology to simulate, monitor, and control smart home devices for improved automation and predictive insights. By integrating virtual representations of home devices and sensors, this project enables users to gain real-time visibility, automate routines, and anticipate maintenance needs, creating a smarter and more proactive living environment.

Features include device digital twins, real-time home monitoring, remote control, predictive maintenance, and scalable architecture for extensibility.
